DALE Research

Research overview

DALE studies how community observations, value, commitments, relationships, and context can remain useful across time, teams, programs, and institutions.

Home Origin story Validation environments Publications and notes

Origin

DALE began with the observation that communities often retain knowledge longer than institutions retain memory.

Validation

DALE is being tested across fellowship ecosystems, social enterprise networks, grassroots communities, civic participation platforms, and community-led development programs.

Public summaries

Public research pages describe the question, environments, and learning agenda without exposing restricted canon or implementation materials.
