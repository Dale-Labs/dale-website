What SEC is
Purpose & Canonical Role

SEC is the enterprise synthesis and coherence layer of the DALE architecture. It organizes traceable structural outputs into enterprise operating architectures, flow systems, stewardship structures, continuity systems, delivery architectures, and scale and governance readiness structures.


SEC does not begin with ideas. It begins with structured system signal already processed through CoS, ECOA, ARA, and Runner. Every enterprise structure it produces must be traceable to upstream system conditions.


?"SEC does not invent coherence. It surfaces whether coherence exists, where it is fragile, and what remains unresolved."
SEC involves
What SEC Does
  • Organizing traceable enterprise structures
  • Mapping value, impact, money, data, and decision flows
  • Identifying operational tensions and dependencies
  • Structuring enterprise operating architecture
  • Exposing bottlenecks and continuity risks
  • Preparing structures for stewardship and formalization
  • Preserving unresolved conditions explicitly
SEC does not involve
What SEC Does Not Do
  • Inventing enterprise ideas detached from system reality
  • Producing polished investor narratives
  • Assuming coherence without traceability
  • Replacing CoS, ECOA, ARA, or Runner
  • Hiding fragility to make the model appear stronger
  • Treating missingness as complete resolution
Why SEC exists
Origin & Motivation

SEC emerged from repeated observation that many enterprises � especially in social impact and localization contexts � fail not because of weak intentions, but because the relationship between value, impact, money, governance, leadership, delivery, and continuity is never structurally mapped.

Traditional failure pattern
What Gets Separated
Revenue from impact  �  Delivery from governance  �  Growth from continuity  �  Data from decision-making. This creates systems that appear functional externally but become fragile under operational stress.
SEC purpose
What SEC Was Developed to Do
Make enterprise coherence observable and traceable before scale, funding, or formalization occurs. Extend the DALE architecture from observing systems (CoS) into synthesizing stewardable operating systems. Expose tensions before they become system failure.

Activation logic
SEC Activation Conditions

SEC is NOT automatically activated. Runner determines the required realization pathway based on the resolved structural conditions of the system. No practitioner decision overrides the realization type determination from Runner.

!SEC does not assume enterprise complexity beyond what the system requires. If structural resolution can occur without enterprise creation, the process may transition directly from Runner to CBC.
Realization types
Runner Realization Type Tags
RT-SEC Enterprise realization required � SEC activates
RT-CBC Governance formalization required � go directly to CBC
RT-H Hybrid � both SEC and CBC required
RT-U Realization pathway unresolved � do not proceed
Conditions
When SEC Activates vs. Bypasses to CBC
SEC activates when Runner indicates that:
  • Value realization requires enterprise operating architecture
  • Impact continuity requires enterprise operating architecture
  • Operational coordination requires enterprise operating architecture
  • Resourcing requires enterprise operating architecture
  • Stewardship or delivery requires enterprise operating architecture
Direct Runner ? CBC (bypass SEC) when:
  • Resolution through negotiated governance without enterprise creation
  • Resolution through benefit formalization without enterprise creation
  • Resolution through contractual accountability without enterprise creation
  • Resolution through institutional restructuring without enterprise creation
Process structure
Seven-Phase Process Overview

The SEC process moves through seven sequential phases. Each produces a named output that feeds the next phase. No phase produces free-form analysis � every output must be traceable to upstream structural inputs.

?Phases cannot be skipped. The CBC Transition Package (CTP) requires completion of Phases 1�6. No enterprise structures may be produced without traceable upstream inputs.

Enterprise flows
Four Mandatory Enterprise Flows (Phase 3)

All four flows must be mapped. Absence of a flow is a structural finding, not a skip. Flow coherence is required for enterprise continuity.

Money Flow
v30
Resource movement � how resources enter, move through, and exit the enterprise. Covers revenue, grants, in-kind contributions, redistribution.
Impact Flow
v31
Change movement � how the enterprise produces and sustains impact. Theory of change pathways, outcome chains.
Data Flow
v32
Information movement � how information is captured, processed, used, and fed back into the enterprise system.
Decision Flow
v33
Authority movement � how decisions are made and by whom. Formal and informal decision structures. Where authority resides and how it moves.
?Flow intersections are where reinforcement or fracture occurs � both must be recorded. Flow tensions are structural findings, not problems to be eliminated at Phase 3.
Coherence assessment
Eight Coherence States

Every enterprise structure receives a coherence state. RES-F (Fragile) and RES-U (Unresolved) are valid and expected outputs � they must not be forced to RES-C (Coherent) where tensions exist.

?SEC does not fabricate coherence. Do not force RES-C where tensions are unresolved. Fragility is a structural finding � preserve it.

Constraints
What Outputs Can and Cannot Represent
Valid output representations
  • Traceable enterprise structure derived from upstream system conditions
  • Coherence assessment of existing structural conditions
  • Flow mapping of observable enterprise movement
  • Explicit record of unresolved tensions and fragility
  • Downstream intake package for CBC governance formalization
Invalid output representations
  • Enterprise ideas generated independently from system reality
  • Fabricated coherence where tensions are unresolved
  • Resolved conditions where structural missingness persists
  • Outputs without traceable upstream source paths
  • Governance formalization (that is CBC's role)

Architecture Versioning
This is v1.0.0 of the SEC canonical spec. The seven-phase structure, coherence state taxonomy (RES-C through RES-CR), realization type logic (RT-SEC/RT-CBC/RT-H/RT-U), and unresolved condition preservation rule are fixed for this version.
Traceability Guarantee
Every SEC Structure is Traceable
No SEC output may exist without traceable inputs from CoS, ECOA, ARA, and Runner. The TracePath field in every output record preserves the full synthesis path from source observation to final output. Unresolved conditions from Phase 1 are carried through all seven phases.
